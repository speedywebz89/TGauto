Mission

Challenging Market

Posted by Tyler Gonzalez | July 4, 2022

In todays market finding there exotic cars gets more and more challenging. I scour the world looking for high-end exotic cars. With inflation and the unpredictable economy many car collectors are holding their inventory and not selling. We Are forunate enough to have such a large client base that many dealers and collectors are pleased to work with us.

The job is simple and stress free, requiring primarily that our associates visit dealerships, develop relationships with sales and/or management staff, and obtain reasonable discounts on specific automobiles. TG Auto Solutions supplies all funding for the acquisitions, so our agents can be as efficient as possible.
